Visit Abbottabad

A gateway to paradise, granting access to the breathtaking beauty of northern Pakistan.

Top three points in Abbottabad

Ilyasi masjid

The mosque's aesthetics incorporate Islamic, British and Sikh architectural influences. Its most prominent feature is its tall minaret which is instantly recognizable.

Shimla Hill

Shimla is famous for its stunning natural beauty, colonial architecture, and old-world charm. Sun rising or setting amidst the hill range at the horizon.

Lady garden

The park was established soon after the foundation of Abbottabad town (January 1853) by Major James Abbott.
